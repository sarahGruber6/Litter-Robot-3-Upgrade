Litter Robot 3 Tracking

--- INPUT/OUTPUTS ---
15V DC Input -> Control Board (CB)
- barrel jack to 2 pin PWR/GND
CB <-> LED and closed circuit indicator board
- 2 pin PWR/GND
CB <-> Drawer Full Indicator (DFI) boards
- 4 pin PWR/GND/SIGs
CB <-> Weight Close Switch
- 2 pin PWR/GND
CB -> 12V Brushed DC Motor
- 2 pin PWR/GND

--- PLAN ---
Replace/Upgrade:
Control Board - Replace
LED - Upgrade w/Camera
- 2 pin to 4 pin (PWR/GND/SPI)
Weight Switch - Upgrade to load cell
- 2 pin to 4 pin (PWR/GND/SPI)

--- DETAILED COMPONENTS ---
LED Bonnet Board:
OEM is just pwr, gnd, 3 LEDs w/resistors. 
- Upgrade with new board, more info under Camera_Board

DFI Sensor:
OEM is a pair one IR LED emitter board and one IR receivers board. Detects if beams are blocked. Need to measure to detect if outputs are digital/serial.

Motor:
OEM is a 12V brushed DC motor giving measurements of .1A at 12V and .2A if it encounters resistance
- using H-Bridge driver on CB

--- DETAILED INFO ---
Control Board:
ESP32-S2 MCU
DRV8231ADSG H-Bridge Motor Driver
Buck Converter 15V->5V
LDO 5V->3.3V


Camera Board:
No IR filtered OV2640 Camera module
ESP32-S2 MCU
white LEDs for nightlight
850nM IR LEDs for night vision

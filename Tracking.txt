--- DETAILED COMPONENTS ---
Control Board:
OEM has 1x 4 pin, 1x 6 pin, and 1x 8 pin DF11 headers
4 Pin: DFI
	1-Blue, 2-Black, 3-Red, 4-Yellow
6 Pin: PWR, Battery, Motor
	1-White(PWR+), 2-Black(PWR-), 3-Green(Motor+), 4-Black(Batt-), 5-Brown(Motor-), 6-Red(Batt+)
8 Pin: Hall Effect, LEDs, Weight
	1-Green(HE PWR), 2-Yellow(HE1), 3-Orange(HE2), 4-Black(HE GND), 5-Blue(Weight+), 6-Black(Weight-), 7-Purple(LED+), 8-Black(LED-)

LED Bonnet Board:
OEM is just pwr, gnd, 3 LEDs w/resistors. 4 pin but only uses 2.
- Upgrade with new board, more info under Camera_Board

Hall Effect Sensors:
one pair of PWR/GNDs spliced for each sensor, then one sig for each

DFI Sensor:
OEM is a pair one IR LED emitter board and one IR receivers board. Detects if beams are blocked. Need to measure to detect if outputs are digital/serial.

Motor:
OEM is a 12V brushed DC motor giving measurements of .1A at 12V and .2A if it encounters resistance
- using H-Bridge driver on CB
